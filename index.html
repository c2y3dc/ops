I

The PI System connects sensor-based data, operations and the people who rely on data to manage process efficiency, asset health, quality and resource management. The PI System works through server-based technology and allows your data – no matter how old or new it is – to be instantly accessible to your users wherever they are. As a result, you achieve end to end visibility across the enterprise.
